From 2015 to 2020 he was a member of the technical staff at MIT Lincoln Laboratory. He joined Northeasterns Electrical and Computer Engineering department in January 2021. His research interests include piezoelectric MEMS, acousto-optic and acousto-electric signal processing devices, oscillator-based computing, nanofabrication techniques and heterogeneous material integration.
