Alumni Profiles Search form Search You are hereHomePeopleField Faculty Francesca Molinari Francesca Molinari Professor Economics Francesca Molinari is a professor in the economics department. Her research focuses on econometric theory and applied econometrics. She got her PhD in Economics from Northwestern University and her BS in Economics from the Universita degli Studi di Torino.
