Footer Aidong Zhang William Wulf Faculty Fellow and Professor, Interim CS Department ChairComputer Science, Biomedical Engineering, and Data Science Home Find Faculty Aidong Zhang Bio PhD, Purdue 1994 Aidong Zhang develops machine learning and data science approaches to modeling and analysis of structured and unstructured data with a variety of applications, especially biomedical applications. Dr. Zhang is a William Wulf Faculty Fellow and Professor of Computer Science, with a joint appointmentinthe Department of Biomedical Engineering andSchool of Data Scienceat University of Virginia. Her research interests focus onmachine learning, data mining, bioinformatics and health informatics.
