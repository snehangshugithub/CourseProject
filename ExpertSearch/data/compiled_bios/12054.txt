People Dr Liang Hu People Dr Liang Hu Lecturer School of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering (CSEE) Email l.hu@essex.ac.uk Location 1NW.3.13, Colchester Campus Academic support hours Friday 12:00-14:00, Zoom Meeting ID: 747 702 3713 Profile Research Teaching and supervision Publications Contact Profile Research Teaching and supervision Publications Contact Profile Biography Dr Liang Hu is a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in the School of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering, as part of Essex Robotics Group, University of Essex, UK. He received a PhD degree in Computer Science from Brunel University London in 2016. From 2016 to 2018, he worked as a Postdoctoral researcher at Queen's University Belfast and subsequently at Loughborough University, in the UK. Before joining the University of Essex, he was a Lecturer with the School of Computer Science and Informatics, De Montfort University, UK. His research interests include safe autonomy, Bayesian filtering and learning, control theory and applications, and cyber-physical systems. He is a Senior Member of the IEEE and has served as an associate editor for a number of journals such as the International Journal of Systems Science, IEEE Access, and IET Cyber-systems and Robotics.
