Hariri Building 595 Commonwealth Avenue Boston, MA 02215 Download CV Leroy Gonsalves is an Assistant Professor of Management and Organizations in the Questrom School of Business at Boston University. He holds a Ph.D. in Organizational Behavior from Harvard Business School. He teaches courses on People Analytics in the undergraduate, MBA and MSBA programs. Prof. Gonsalves studies work and occupations, focusing on how professionals manage temporal challenges such as work-life tensions, disruptive events, and short-term performance pressures.
