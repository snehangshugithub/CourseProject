His interests include the intersection of technology and society, and in particular, ethical and social issues involving data storage, processing, and management. Prof. Amers research focuses on systems topics, particularly data storage systems, operating systems, and distributed systems. His work also includes alternative and upcoming storage technologies, and their most practical and effective applications. He holds a doctorate in Computer Science from, and remains associated with theStorage Systems Research Centerat, theUniversity of California, Santa Cruz.
