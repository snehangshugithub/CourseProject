Research Interests:Prof. Underhill's research focuses on using a combined theoretical, computational, and experimental approach to understand problems in engineering, physics, and biology. Examples include advanced material development, separation and manipulation of biological molecules, nonequilibrium statistical mechanics, and biochemical pathways for quorum sensing and chemotaxis.
