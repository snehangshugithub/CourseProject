Professor of Computer ScienceAddress:AKW 308A, 51 Prospect Street, New Haven, CT 06511203.436.9450lin.zhong@yale.eduWebsiteLin Zhong received his B.S and M.S. from Tsinghua University and Ph.D. from Princeton University. From 2005 t0 2019, he was with Rice University. At Yale, he leads the Efficient Computing Lab to make computing, communication, and interfacing more efficient and effective. He and his students received the best paper awards from ACM MobileHCI, IEEE PerCom, and ACM MobiSys (3), and ACM ASPLOS. He is a recipient of the NSF CAREER Award, the Duncan Award from Rice University, and the RockStar Award from ACM SIGMOBILE. He is a Fellow of IEEE. More information about his research can be found at http://www.yecl.org.
