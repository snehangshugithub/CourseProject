All tenure-track assistant, associate, full-time professors are eligible, and we're looking for professors with outstanding passion and research in CSE ,Science field. Applicants have to have Ph.D degree at the time of their application, and nationalities, gender, age doesn't matter. Since 1975, Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National university have pursued world-class research and education for 40 years. Currently 35 professors are here with 400 undergraduate and 350 graduate students and we are constantly growing. Research and Education are taking place in Theory of Computation, AI(Artificial Intelligence) , Computer System, System software, Embedded System, Data Science, Network, Programming Language, Graphics, Vision, HCI, Bioinformatics, Machine Learning, etc. field. Our Faculty recruitment is applicable for professors who can lead future-oriented research in every CSE field.
