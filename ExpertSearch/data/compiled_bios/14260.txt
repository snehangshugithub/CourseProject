Eng.Room: STE 4045Office: 613-562-5800 ext. 2178Work E-mail: ebouende@uOttawa.ca Biography Dr. Bouendeu is a professional engineer with an entrepreneurial mind-set and member of the teaching staff at the Centre for Entrepreneurship and Engineering Design (CEED) at the University of Ottawa. He obtained a Ph.D. from the University of Freiburg in Germany in the area of Micro-Energy Harvesting Engineering. Prior joining the University of Ottawa in 2018, he was the renewable energy project manager at Ottawa Renewable Energy Co-op (OREC), co-founder of Environs Energy International and Bouendeu CleanTech Inc., test engineer at Moeller Holding GmbH in Germany, part-time professor at Cit Collegiale in Ottawa, lecturer and research scientist at the University of Freiburg in Germany. Dr. Bouendeu is expert in micro-energy technologies, thick film electroluminescent structures, solid-state lighting and renewable energies. His research and interest fields include photovoltaics engineering, micro-energy harvesting, thick-film electroluminescent structures, LED lighting, clean energy storage systems, energy management, electrical power, knowledge dissemination and education. Dr. Bouendeu is senior member of the Association of German Engineers (VDI) and EU-Engineers and member of Professional Engineer Ontario (PEO).
