She has been part of the co-op department since 2013 and has worked with graduate students since 2015. Her previous experience includes working at Suffolk Universitys Performing Arts Office and Presidents Office, Berklee College of Musics Office of Experiential Learning, DePaul University Law Career Services, and the University of the Incarnate Words Career Services Office. Her educational background includes a bachelors degree in English from the University of the Incarnate Word, a masters degree in Writing and Publishing from DePaul University, a masters degree in Higher Education Administration from Suffolk University, and a Doctor of Education from Northeastern University. Her dissertation was entitled International Student Experiences with Cooperative Education.
