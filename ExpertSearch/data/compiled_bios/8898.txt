Biography Carol Tilley teaches courses in comics readers advisory, media literacy, and youth services librarianship. Her scholarship focuses on the intersection of young people, comics, and libraries, particularly in the United States during the mid-twentieth century. Tilley's comics research has been featured in theNew York Times, BoingBoing, WNYC'sTakeaway, and other media outlets. A frequent speaker at academic conferences as well as popular comics events, Tilley has served as an Eisner Award judge (2016) and presidentof the Comics Studies Society (2018-2019).
