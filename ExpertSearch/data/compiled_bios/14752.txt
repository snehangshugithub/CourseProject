Mathematics, University of Arkansas B.S. Economics, Instituto Tecnologico Autonomo de Mexico Biography Zdenka Guadarrama, Ph.D., is an associate professor and chair of the department of mathematics. She is passionate about mathematics education and outreach, with her work currently focusing on mathematics curriculum development through inquiry, and the intersections of mathematics with other fields, particularly the arts.
