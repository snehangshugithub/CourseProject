Resources As a valued member of Georgia State University's graduate faculty, the Office of Graduate Services is committed to providing you with a variety of tools needed to support graduate student success. This page includes a wealth of information on current graduate student policies, procedures and resources available. If you need assistance, please feel free to contact your department's designated advisor in the Office of Graduate Services.
