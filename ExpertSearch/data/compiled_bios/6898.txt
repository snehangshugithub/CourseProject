Greene Center, 3227 Broadway, Quad 6C New YorkNY10027Email:[emailprotected]Website:https://www.abdus-saboorlab.com/Cell & Molecular BiologyComputational BiologyDevelopmental BiologyGenetics & GenomicsNeurobiologyNew PhD Program FacultyAbdus-Saboor Lab MembersShort Research Description:Genes and neural circuits for pain and touch behavior Full Research Description:Our research focuses on a long-standing question, how does the nervous system encode a soft gentle caress, versus an itchy mosquito bite, versus a harsh painful stimulation? And importantly, how do encoding principles change when the baseline state changes, such as during chronic pain. To accomplish this, we use systems neuroscience, computation, genetics, and mathematics to link animal behavior with genes and neural circuits.
