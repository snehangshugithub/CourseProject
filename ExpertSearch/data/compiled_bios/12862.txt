Alumni Achievement Award Civil Engineering Alumni Achievement Award Civil Engineering at Purdue has a long history of educating outstanding engineers and the Civil Engineering Alumni Achievement Award gives the School the opportunity to recognize the career accomplishments of some of our most influential graduates. Graduates of our program have been on the forefront of creativity, shaping the landscapes of many great cities. Our graduates have gone on to be instrumental in the creation of major world landmarks such as the Hoover Dam, the Chunnel beneath the English Channel, and the Panama Canal. Our people are plugged into solving environmental issues that improve our quality of life. They propel the development of infrastructure that underpins the worlds economy. And they are a powerful presence in worldwide disaster relief. Their accomplishments have been nothing short of exemplary. The primary criterion in the selection process is excellence. The award applauds career achievements and contributions to the civil engineering profession. The recipients are selected by a faculty committee and approved by the entire civil engineering faculty. With the Civil Engineering Alumni Achievement Awards, we reaffirm our commitment to be the nations preeminent civil engineering program by reflecting on what some of our most distinguished graduates have accomplished in their illustrious careers.
