Keen Misbah Keen, MD, MBI, MPH Professor and Executive Vice Chair, Family Medicine Adjunct Professor, Biomedical Informatics and Medical Education mkeen@uw.edu Website Interests: Medical Education especially decentralized clinical education. Clinical Informatics Education. Background: I serve as the Executive Vice Chair in the Department of Family Medicine (DFM) at University of Washington School of Medicine (UWSOM) and oversee the operation of Family Medicine Medical Student Education section and the MEDEX PA program. I also serve as a Family Medicine Liaison for our BIME Clinical Informatics Fellowship. Research: Communication skills. Teaching: Co-director of Family Medicine Clerkship. Other Roles: Co-Chair of the Patient Care Phase Committee at the UW School of Medicine.
