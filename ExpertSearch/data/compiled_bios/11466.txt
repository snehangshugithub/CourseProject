Professor Lejeunes background is in the area of computational mechanics and computational biomechanics. Her research is focused on leveraging the state of the art in computational mechanics to investigate multiscale emergent behavior in biological systems and inform patient-specific medical protocols. Current areas of research involve integrating data-driven and physics based computational models and predicting the mechanical behavior of highly heterogeneous soft tissue.
