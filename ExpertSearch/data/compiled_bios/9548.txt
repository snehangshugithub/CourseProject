Hariri Building 595 Commonwealth Avenue Boston, MA 02215 Download CV Zvi Bodie is the Norman and Adele Barron Professor of Management at Boston University until 2015 when he retired. He holds a PhD from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and has served on the finance faculty at the Harvard Business School and MIT's Sloan School of Management. His textbook, Investments, coauthored by Alex Kane and Alan Marcus is the market leader and is used in the certification programs of the CFA Institute and the Society of Actuaries. In 2007 the Retirement Income Industry Association gave him their Lifetime Achievement Award for applied research.
