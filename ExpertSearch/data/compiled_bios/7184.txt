She was a Hubble Postdoctoral Fellow at the Carnegie Observatories in Pasadena, CA. Professor French joined the faculty at UIUC in 2020. Research InterestsGalaxy evolution Transient astronomy Optical and radio observations Research DescriptionProfessor French uses multi-wavelength observations to study how galaxies evolve over time and co-evolve with their supermassive black holes.
