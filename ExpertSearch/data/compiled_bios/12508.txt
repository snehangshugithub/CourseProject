She joined Santa Clara University in January 1994. Before that, she was on faculty of the Center for Advanced Computer Studies, University of SW Louisiana for three and half years. She received Research Initiation Award in 1991 and Career Award in 1995 form National Science Foundation. She was a Clare Boothe Luce Professor between 1994 and 2000. Her research interests include parallel processing, computer architecture, parallelizing compiler, algorithm theory and non-linear optimization.
