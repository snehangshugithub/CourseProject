He has completed his Ph. D. in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the National Institute of Technology Rourkela India. He received his Master of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from National Institute of Technology Rourkela, India in 2014. He has done his Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering fromUttar Pradesh Technical University Lucknow India. He was an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering: Visakhapatnam India from August 2017 to January 2021. He has qualified Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) two times in 2007 and 2011. His current research interests includes Formal Methods, Design Patterns, Security Patterns, Ontology, and Machine Learning. He served as a technical reviewer of several international journals and conferences.
