Green Street Urbana, IL 61801 (217) 244-7303 vddries@illinois.edu Research AreasAlgebraLogic Edit Your Profile Professor Emeritus BiographyLou van den Dries joined the department in 1986. He was an invited ICM speaker in 1990 and 2018, has been a correspondent of the Royal Dutch Academy of Sciences since 1993, and he has been a Professor in the Center for Advanced Study at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign since 1998. Most recently, he presented the Tarski Lecture at the University of California at Berkeley in spring 2017. Research DescriptionModel theory and its applications. Applications of logic to algebra.
