He is currently a postdoctoral research associate in the Institute for Experiential Robotics at Northeastern University. His research interests span the domains of mixed reality, shared control and representation learning for human-robot interaction. All of these topics coincide with his overarching ambition to advance assistive robotics through transparent and seamless user experiences.
