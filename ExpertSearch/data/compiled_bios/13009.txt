Hommersand, Max H. May 4, 2011 Hommersand, Max H. Professor (Emeritus) hommersand@bio.unc.edu 407 Coker Hall (919) 962-6938 (office) Lab Website Publications Max Hommersands area of research is the morphology, systematics and biography of the marine algae, with emphasis on the red algae. He has a world-wide collection of over thirty thousand specimens of marine algae for use in developmental studies. Research facilities include a Zeiss photomicroscope for light microscopy, three large culture rooms for growing algae, and other facilities for investigating the evolutionary biology and phylogeny of the marine algae.
