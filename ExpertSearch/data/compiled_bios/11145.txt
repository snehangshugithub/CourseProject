His research has appeared in many refereed journals and conference proceedings, such as IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering (TKDE), Real Estate Economics, ACM Transactions on Knowledge Discovery from Data (TKDD), ACM Transactions on Management Information Systems (TMIS), and ACM SIGKDD International Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (KDD). He has served on the organization and program committees of numerous international conferences, such as KDD, ICDM, SDM, CIKM, and INFORMS annual meetings. He is a senior member of the IEEE and the ACM.
