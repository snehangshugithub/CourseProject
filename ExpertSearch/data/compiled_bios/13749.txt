His research focus is on the intersection of computation and statistics. Professor Xu seeks to understand the deep interplay between statistical optimality and computational complexity in high-dimensional statistical inference problems. He has been working on sharp performance analysis of semidefinite programming relaxations and belief propagation for community detection. Professor Xu teaches Decision Analytics and Modeling.
