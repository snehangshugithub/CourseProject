Neilan Professor, PhD Dr. Neilan's research interests include computational and applied mathematics with an emphasis on the mathematical theory of the finite element method. Specific topics include numerical approximations for fully nonlinear problems, stable finite element pairs preserving physical quantities, and discontinuous Galerkin methods.
