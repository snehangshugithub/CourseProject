My primary research interest is on the development of systems based on learning and the applications of cryptography to empower and protect users in this tech era. I am also intrigued to research new areas of computer science to maximize business, technology, and societal impact. I am an ABD doctoral candidate in Computer Science at University of Louisiana at Lafayette. And I received my M.S. in Computer Science from the same university in 2016.
