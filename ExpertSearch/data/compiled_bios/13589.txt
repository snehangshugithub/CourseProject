Personal profile Research interestsProfile Dr Fullekrug received his Postgraduate Certificate for Teaching and Learning in Higher Education from the University of Bath, Habilitation from the Johannn-Wolfgang Goethe Universitt Frankfurt am Main, postdoctoral experience from Stanford University and PhD from the Universitt of Gttingen. Research Dr Fullekrug's research work involves the measurement, analysis and interpretation of radio waves to study the electrodynamic properties of the atmosphere, near-Earth space.
