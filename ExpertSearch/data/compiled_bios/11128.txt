He has presented at finance and economic conferences around the world such as the China International Conference in Finance and the American Economic Association and has been published in finance and economic journals including the World Economy and the Journal of Management, Policy, and Practice.Dr. Wynter completed his PhD in finance from Ohio State in 2014 and previously worked as an assistant professor of finance at the University of Illinois at Chicago where he received the Deans Letter of Outstanding Teaching in 2017. Most recently he served as a visiting scholar with the U.S. Security and Exchange Commissions Office of Litigation Economics from 2018 to 2020.
