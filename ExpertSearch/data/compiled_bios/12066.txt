Ying Zhu's Homepage Ying Zhu Assistant Professor Faculty of Business and Information Technology cross-appointed in Faculty of Engineering and Applied Science University of Ontario Institute of Technology Research interests Design of algorithms to solve problems in distributed systems, using theoretical tools of machine learning, stochastic processes and information theory. Sychronization of data between mobile hand-held devices and the server cloud in a shared network system. Performance optimization in distributed systems such as peer-topeer overlay networks and wireless networks. Pervasive systems involving sensors and RFID technology. Application of theoretical tools from machine learning to solve problems in various application-layer services residing on top of heterogeneous network infrastructures, including network traffic data analysis through visualization, automatic anomaly detection in medical monitoring, and forensic analysis of network attack events.
