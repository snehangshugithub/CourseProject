Audience Tools Search Menu Faculty > Terri Eccles Terri Eccles CV Guest Faculty Member teccles@uw.edu Specializations Product Strategy Product-Led Organizations and Culture Leadership Development Courses IMT 587 - Principles Of Information Project Management Biography Terri serves as guest faculty at the University of Washington Information School specializing in product & project management. She has over 15 years of experience in product strategy and execution. She was Director of Digital Product at Capital One and the Director of Digital Web at Premera. In addition to her work at the Information School, she does strategy & culture consulting for startups and product/tech teams. She specializes in high consumer impact spaces like fintech, healthcare, government, and mission-led startups. She believes product leaders can lead the way in developing tech that solves our world's largest challenges and puts humans (customers and employees) at the heart of their strategies.
