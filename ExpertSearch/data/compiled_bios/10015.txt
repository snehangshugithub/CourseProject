Operating Systems and System Programming, TuTh 3:30PM - 4:59PM, Valley Life Sciences 2050 Biography Professor Joseph is a Chancellor's Professor at UC Berkeley. His primary research interests are in Genomics, Secure Machine Learning, Datacenters, mobile/distributed computing, and wireless communications (networking and telephony). He is part of the RISE Lab.
