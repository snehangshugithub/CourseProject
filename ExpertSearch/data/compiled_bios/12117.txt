Nathan's research interests include personalization; optimization, especially under uncertainty; causal inference; sequential decision making; credible and robust inference; and algorithmic fairness. He holds a PhD in Operations Research from MIT as well as a BA in Mathematics and a BS in Computer Science both from UC Berkeley.
