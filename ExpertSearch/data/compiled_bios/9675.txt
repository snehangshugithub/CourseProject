Established in 2000, CEPAC draws its membership from both professional practitionersmany of whom are UIC alumniand civil engineering faculty members. Its ranks includeleaders in the environmental, geotechnical, structures, transportation, and water resourcesfields and represent private companies and government agencies throughoutthe Chicago region.
