Luria Professor of Biology Bob Sauer studies intracellular proteolytic machines responsible for protein-quality control and homeostasis. 617-253-3163 Phone 68-533A Office bobsauer@mit.edu Email Lab Website Sarah Wylie Assistant 617-253-9395 Assistant Phone Education PhD, 1979, Harvard University BA, 1972, Biophysics, Amherst College Research Summary We study the relationship between protein structure, function, sequence and folding. We focus on the molecular machines that degrade or remodel proteins, targeting proteins for these ATP-dependent reactions. Our experimental tools include biochemistry and single-molecule biophysics, structural biology, protein design and engineering, and molecular genetics.
