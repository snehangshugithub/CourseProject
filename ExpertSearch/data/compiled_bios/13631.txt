He develops mathematical and computational methods to address questions that arise in the study of the 3D structure of chromosomes. Most recently and in response to the COVID-19 pandemic he is developing new mathematical and data science methods to study the evolution of the SARS-COV2 variants. More information about his research efforts, teaching, and publications can be found on his main profile website, linked above.
