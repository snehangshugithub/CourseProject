Leadership Development Graduate Student Fellowships Awards Home>>Faculty>>2021>>March>>12>>Yoon Kim Faculty Browse All faculty Yoon Kim Yoon Kim will join the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science as an Assistant Professor in July.Currently a research scientist at the MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab, Kim received a PhD in Computer Science from Harvard, an MS in Data Science from NYU, an MA in Statistics from Columbia, and dual BA degrees in Mathematics and Economics from Cornell. Kims research focuses on machine learning and natural language processing. He is the recipient of a Google Fellowship.
