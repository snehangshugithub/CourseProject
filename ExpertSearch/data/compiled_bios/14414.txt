Area(s) Structural Engineering Affiliation(s) Bowen Laboratory Education PhD, California Institute of Technology, 2000 MSCE, California Institute of Technology, 1994 BSCE, Middle East Technical University, 1993 Research Summary Earthquake and blast engineering, performance-based structural engineering frameworks, modal testing and analysis, structural health-monitoring, large-scale structural analysis and simulation, and engineering seismology. Selected Research Projects Middle East Technical University, Seismic Vulnerability Assessment of Select Historic Masonry Buildings in Fatih District of Istanbul, Turkey, Five Months (2010), Irfanoglu. National Science Foundation, Collaborative Research: the Learning Bridge, 2009-2011, Irfanoglu, National Science Foundation/NEES Operations, 2010-2014, Irfangolu w/ Ramirez, Fossum, Eigenmann, Hacker, Bahchi and Brophy. U.S. Department of State & WJE Assoc., Inc., A Study on the Seismic Risk of Four Buildings in Istanbul, Turkey, One month 2009, Irfanoglu. U.S. Department of State & WJE Assoc., Inc., A Study on the Seismic Risk of Two Buildings in Istanbul, Turkey, Two months 2009, Irfanoglu. Honors & Awards American Institute of Steel Construction (AISC) IDEAS2 Presidential Award of Excellence, 2008. California Institute of Technology Graduate Student Council, Teaching Assistant Award: nominated by the students of the Engineering Mathematics course, academic year 1997-98. Harold Hellwig Fellowship in Structural Engineering (1993-94). Recognition for Academic Excellence (summa cum laude and top rank), Department of Civil Engineering, Middle East Technical University, 1993. Recognition for Academic Excellence, Turkish Chamber of Civil Engineers, 1993.
