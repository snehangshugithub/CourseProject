Thompson Distinguished Lecture Series Close Body People RICE STATISTICS > People Our prestigious faculty are global leaders in the field of statistics and have won awards and recognition from national and international professional societies for their contributions to the discipline. They have been recognized for their teaching, mentoring and early in their careers, for their promise as valuable members of the community of statisticians. Among our faculty are several fellows of the American Statistical Association (ASA), American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS) and the Institute of Mathematical Statistics (IMS), as well as elected members of the Royal Statistical Society.
