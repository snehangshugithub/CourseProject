People Dr Shoaib Ehsan People Dr Shoaib Ehsan Senior Research Fellow School of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering (CSEE) Email sehsan@essex.ac.uk Location 3A.529, Colchester Campus Profile Research Teaching and supervision Publications Grants and funding Contact Profile Research Teaching and supervision Publications Grants and funding Contact Profile Biography Dr Shoaib Ehsan is an experienced researcher, having won major research accolades, including the British Machine Vision Association's Sullivan Doctoral Thesis Prize (2013), and recognition as a 'Researcher with Exceptional Promise' by the Royal Academy of Engineering (2014). He is also a Senior Member of the IEEE. He has been involved in several EPSRC, ESRC and EU projects and has made significant contributions with his expertise in multiple fields, especially computer vision, embedded systems, robotics and artificial intelligence. Dr Ehsan has a strong track record of inclusive leadership/co-leadership of large international and national research projects and networks. For example, he is currently a Researcher Co-Investigator for the National Centre for Nuclear Robotics (NCNR), which is a 42M initiative by EPSRC, research institutions, industrial collaborators and investment partners. Dr Ehsan is co-leading a team of 12+ research students and post-docs at the University of Essex.
