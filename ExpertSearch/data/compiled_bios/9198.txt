Joined Yale Faculty 2019. Associate Professor of Computer Science Address:AKW 213, 51 Prospect Street, New Haven, CT 06511abhishek@cs.yale.eduWebsiteAbhishek works at the boundary of computer systems architecture and systems software. His focus is on both classical systems that process large amounts of data (like data centers)and also emerging systems for medical applications (in particular, the brain sciences). Abhishek received the Chancellors Award for Faculty Excellence in Research at Rutgers University, where he was on the faculty from 2010 to 2018. Before that, he completed his PhD from Princeton University, where he was awarded the Gordon Wu Prize, and his BEng from McGill University, where he received the British Association Medal for Great Distinction.
