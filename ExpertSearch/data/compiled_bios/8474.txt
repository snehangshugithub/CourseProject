You are hereHomeAcademicsFacultyFaculty Directory Shichao Liu Civil, Environmental, & Architectural Engineering Assistant Professor Affiliated Department or Office:Civil & Environmental Engineering Education:Postdoc University of California, Berkeley 2017PhD The University of Texas at Austin 2014BS/MS Tianjin University, China 2009 Prof. Liu is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI). His research focuses on built and urban environment, occupant-building-environment interaction, and integrated design for sustainable and healthy buildings. Prof. Liu envisions his research promoting built sustainability and occupant well-being through the interface among building science, information science, public health, and social science.
