Prior to coming to UW Bothell, she was a tenured associate professor at the Department of Computer Science, University of Montana. Her research interests include distributed multimedia database systems, data mining, machine learning, and their applications on real life problems and interdisciplinary projects. She has published more than 70 refereed papers in international journals, book chapters, and conference proceedings. She received the best paper award from the 15th IEEE International Conference on Information Reuse and Integration, and 2015 IGI Global annual excellence in research journal award. Dr. Chen is currently serving as the secretary and newsletter editor for IEEE Computer Society Technical Committee on Multimedia Computing, the Associate Editor for International Journal of Multimedia Data Engineering and Management, the Program Co-Chair for 2022 IEEE International Conference on Information Reuse and Integration for Data Science. She was the lead co-chair for 2015 IEEE International Symposium on Multimedia, 2017 IEEE International Conference on Multimedia Big Data, 2018 IEEE International Conference on Multimedia & Expo, and 2019 IEEE International Conference on Multimedia Information Processing and Retrieval, and a voting member on the IEEE ICME Steering Committee.
