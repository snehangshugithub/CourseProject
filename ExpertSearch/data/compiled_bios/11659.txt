Click here for more information Civil and Environmental Engineering Hyung-Sool Lee Associate ProfessorContact InformationEmail: h95lee@uwaterloo.caPhone: 519-888-4567 x31095Location: E2 2309WebsiteHyung-Sool LeeGoogle ScholarLinkedInBiography SummaryHyung-Sool Lee is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering and is also the Director of the Waterloo Environmental Biotechnology Lab at the University of Waterloo. His research mainly involves fundamentals and applications of Microbial Electrochemical Cells (MECs) for resource-recovering waste and wastewater treatment, bio-batteries, and biosensors. In addition, Professor Lee has worked for anaerobic membrane bioreactor, dark fermentation, and anaerobic methane oxidation coupled to denitrification. As a result of his research work, Professor Lee has published over 90 journals and conference proceedings and holds 7 patents in total.
