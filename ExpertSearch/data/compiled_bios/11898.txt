He has designed award winning products for clients such as Kryptonite Locks, Burton, Siemens, Ambient Devices, APC, Homax, Boston Scientific, MSI, Prudential, The First Years, and Waterworks. His work has been featured in ID magazine, Innovation, Core77, Maxim, Transworld Skateboarding, Men's Health, The Pittsburgh Tribune Review, The Bellingham Herald and The Boston Globe. Jason earned his Master's of Industrial Design from Pratt Institute and his Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from West Virginia University. Research InterestsProf. Morris has been focusing his research on industrial design history, social purpose innovation,three dimensional form, and creativity.
