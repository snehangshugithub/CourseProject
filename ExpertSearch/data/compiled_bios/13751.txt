Hilton Professor of the Practice in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering Drew Hilton is an Associate Professor of the Practice in Electrical and Computer Engineering, as well as Pratts Director of Innovation in Computing Education. His main focus is on teachingprofessional-level programming skills to ECEs master's students to prepare them for software engineering careers. Professor Hilton also teaches a 3-week introduction to Programming Python forDukes Master in Interdisciplinary Data Science, and Dukes Center for Computational Thinking.He has two Coursera specializations, one in Java, and another in C.
