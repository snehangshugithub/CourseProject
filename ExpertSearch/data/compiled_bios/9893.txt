Alumni Current Student Faculty / Staff First Generation Student Parent Student Veteran Transfer Student Vikings Fan PSU Xiaoyu Song Xiaoyu Song Professor Office FAB 160-15 Phone (503) 725-5398 Email song@ece.pdx.edu Contact me Professor Song received his Ph.D. degree from the University of Pisa, Pisa, Italy, 1991. From 1992 to 1998, he was a Faculty Member with the University of Montreal, Canada. In 1998, he was a Senior Technical Staff with Cadence Design Systems, San Jose, CA. Since 1999, he has been with Portland State University, where he is currently a Professor with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. He was an editor of IEEE Transactions on VLSI Systems and IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems. He was awarded an Intel Faculty Fellowship from 2000 to 2005.
