He has an extensive experience as an online educator, developing and teaching courses on Data Science, Data Analytics, Game Analytics and Data Mining subjects in a number of online undergraduate and graduate programs. Feyzi Bagirov has participated in the creation of the graduate Master of Science in Data Analytics at the University of Maryland University College and was a Founding Director of Data Science program at Becker College (ranked #5 on The Princeton Reviews list as the top-25 undergraduate schools worldwide to study game design for 2015). His experience and expertise include data science/analytics, machine learning, client/server programming, and online learning. His experience also includes rapid prototyping web-based products, customer development, discovery, validation, growth management, and business development for early-stage startups. Feyzi Bagirov has taught Analytics and Data Science at the University of Maryland University College, Southern New Hampshire University, Becker College (authored DATA2001 Data Science Tools and Techniques, GAME4200 Game Analytics courses) and Harrisburg University of Science and Technology. His research interests are Natural Language Processing. Education Master Of Business Administration (M.B.A.), Babson College, Wellesley Hills, MA, USA. Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in International Economics, Azerbaijan University, Baku, Azerbaijan.
