My advisor wasGeorge Karypis.In 2011, I received my M.S. in computer science from Texas State University, under the guidance ofByron J. Gao. Highlights: Research in machine learning, data mining, computational genomics, and high-performance computing. Received the Next Generation Data Scientist award at IEEE DSAA 2016, for work on efficient exact nearest neighbor search methods. Built a supercomputer from scratch, for the research and education benefit of SJSU faculty and students. Passionate about teaching and mentoring students.
