Cordner Distinguished Professor of Money and Financial Markets at UCLA AndersonSchool of Management. He teaches in the Departments of Economics and Public Policy at UCLA and serves as director of the John M. Olin Center for Policy at UCLA Anderson. Concurrently he holds appointments as chairman of the Dumbarton Group, research associate with the National Bureau of Economic Research and adjunct scholar with the American Enterprise Institute.
