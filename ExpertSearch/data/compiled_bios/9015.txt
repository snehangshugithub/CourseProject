She has an MBA from Northeastern University, M.A. in International Relations, and B.A. in International Studies and Sociology from Salve Regina University. Sally previously coordinated projects and managed daily operations for a large engineering department in a satellite-communications company. She has experience in studying, living, and working abroad in South America, Eastern and Western Europe, and Southeast Asia.
