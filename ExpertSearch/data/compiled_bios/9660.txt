He teaches undergraduate and graduate courses in Civil Engineering and in Construction Engineering and Management and oversees the Construction Engineering and Management program. He is a registered Professional Engineer in the US (States of Nevada, Texas, and Wyoming) and in Canada (Provinces of British Columbia, Ontario, and Quebec). He has the industry experience in the fields of structural design of civil infrastructure systems, business administration of heavy civil projects, and project controls of large-scale civil infrastructure for large global Engineering-Procurement and Construction (EPC) firms. He is a Fellow of the American Society of Civil Engineers (ASCE), and serves on the national ASCE committees of Construction Claims Avoidance and Resolution, Construction Research Council, and Forensic Engineering Education. He has a PhD in Civil Engineering from University of Southern California in Los Angeles, California, and holds three Masters degrees in Civil Engineering; Construction Management; and in Business Administration.
