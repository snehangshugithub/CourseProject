Academic research in the College of Business is rigorous and relevant to both the business and academic communities. That is, we conduct academic research that is grounded in solid theory and that addresses practical problems that management faces. Whether it is a focus on how ones construal of the time horizon impacts investment decisions orhow childhood advertising exposure to brand characters can impact product choice into adulthood, our research provides practical solutions for business problems, which is especially important in times of economic uncertainty. The College of Business is proud to announce that our research faculty has grown significantly in the past two decades, and has included joint appointments with several departments at Stony Brook, including the Department of Economics the Department of Political Science. Our faculty members have doctoral degrees from Baruch College, Boston University, Carnegie Mellon University, Cornell University, Long Island University, Northwestern University, Pennsylvania State University, Rutgers University, Sogang University, Stanford University, Stony Brook University, Temple University, The Hebrew University of Jerusalem, The Ohio State University, Tulane University, University of Alberta, University of Arizona, University of British Columbia, University of California, Santa Barbara, University of Chicago, University of Kansas, University of Kentucky, University of Rochester, University of South Florida, University of Virginia, University of Washington, and Yale University. Our faculty are committed to the growth of the College of Business and to extending our research footprint and impact.
