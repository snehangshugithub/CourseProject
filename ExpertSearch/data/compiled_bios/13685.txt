These interfaces includesub-system architectures for neural recording and modulation andanalog circuits for low noise preamplifier, high channel count multiplexer and programmable current sources.Dr. Morizio's current research is focused on biomedical instrumentation intended for neurological, digestive and immune system disorders with the research goal for human clinical translation.He has over 35 years of analog circuit design experience in industry and academics and co-inventor of 8 patents.
