He is particularly interested in active learningespecially with atypical objectivesBayesian optimization, intelligent approaches to approximate Bayesian inference, and Bayesian quadrature. He is also interested in learning problems involving large-scale graph data. Biography Professor Garnett came to WashU in January 2015.
