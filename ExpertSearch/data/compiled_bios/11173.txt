Service Systems; Supply Chain Management; Applied Queueing Theory; Stochastic Modeling Teaching Interests: Operations Management; Supply Chain Analytics Download CV Biography Biography of Mohammad Delasay Mohammad Delasay is an Assistant Professor of Operations Management at the Stony Brook University College of Business since August 2017. Before joining Stony Brook University, he was a Visiting Assistant Professor of Operations Management at the Carnegie Mellon University Tepper School of Business from 2014-2017. He received a PhD in Operations Management from the University of Alberta School of Business in 2014.His research focuses on analyzing and improving the congestion and performance of service and healthcare systems using queueing and stochastic models. He has published papers on these topics in Operations Research, European Journal of Operational Research, Health Care Management Science, and various other journals.He teaches the Operations Management and Supply Chain Management courses at the Stony Brook Ubiversity College of Business.
