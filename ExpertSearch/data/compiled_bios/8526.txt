consists of 32 core faculty and about 60 extended faculty who work closely with 15 staff. Our core and extended faculty have appointments in 24 schools and departments across the University of Washington. The Biomedical and Health Informatics (BHI) student population is currently made up of 7 MS students, 40 PhD students, 4 post-doctoral training fellows, and4 Clinical Informatics fellows. The Online Master of Science in Clinical Informatics & Patient-Centered Technologies (CIPCT) program has an average of 50 active MS students.
