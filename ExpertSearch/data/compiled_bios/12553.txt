the Faculty Strategic Partners of the Faculty The Faculty has a large network of renowned partner universities worldwide. With the objective to strengthen its international profile and to benefit from synergy effects, the Faculty has defined four Universities which have focal areas, structures and interests similar to those of the Faculty, as strategic partners. There are different types of collaboration with these universities, including the formation of a research community, the exchange of teaching staff and students, a double degree program, mutually organised doctoral seminars and scientific conferences.
