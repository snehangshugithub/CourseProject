He has more than 35years of experience from both academia and the wireless (radio and radar) industry, where his work has resulted in numerous products, patents and scientific papers. He has served on both regulatory and standards committees(ETSI and 3GPP) as well served as a consultant to the Swedish National Regulator for Post and Telecommunications (PTS). He is the adviser of close to100 M.Sc. students, 7 licentiate and 3 PhD thesis and has published 100+ journal articles and conference reports.He was KTHs Principal Investigator in theEU (FP7) project on 5G,METIS andhas led multiple Vinnova, KK-foundation and SSF projects. In total he has generated more than $30M for various research projects related to wireless networks, radars, antennas and propagation He is currently a senior researcherin theRadio Systems labanda part time technical expert forIcomera AB while serving as board member ofMedfield Diagnostics AB(Public)and technical board member ofSkysenseAB and Proan AB.
