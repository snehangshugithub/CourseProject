Research Summary Development, integration, calibration, and utilization of wheel-based, UAV-based, airborne, and space borne mobile mapping systems and collected geo-spatial data from onboard imaging and LiDAR sensors for a wide range of applications in smart agriculture, geometric documentation of transportation corridors, environmental monitoring, crash scene reconstruction, and infrastructure inspection.
