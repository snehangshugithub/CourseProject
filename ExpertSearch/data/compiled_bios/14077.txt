Judt The managing director of the Deans Office is in charge of the organizational and personnel management and is jointly responsible for operational management of the Deans Office. In addition to the continuous further development and optimization of structures and processes at the Deans Office, the managing director is responsible for faculty planning in financial and HR matters. She also coordinates matters concerning the appointment and continued employment of professors in the faculty, oversees committee management and manages both internal faculty projects and university-wide projects.
