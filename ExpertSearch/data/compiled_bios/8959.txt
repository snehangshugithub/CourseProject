Brians background is in electrical engineering and statistics specializing in product reliability estimation. Over his 25 year career he has worked in academic research, product development, quality, and consulting in industries such as robotics, packaging, medical devices, and LED lighting. Brian holds a BS in Electrical Engineering and an MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University and an MS in Statistics from Colorado State University.
