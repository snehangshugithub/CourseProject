He received his M.Sc. and Ph.D. from the Royal Institute of Technology in 1999 and 2004 respectively. Mathias'research interests include information security and cybersecurityin combination withsoftware and systemsarchitecture modeling and analysis.In particular the research is applied in the power industry and information systems related to physical process monitoring and control, such as SCADA and Industrial Control Systems. Much of the research revolves around developing formalisms for analyzing security vulnerabilities of system-of-systemsarchitectures trough the means of probabilistic attack/defense graphs. A corenerstone in this work is the Meta Attack Language (MAL). Mathias isthe co-founder anddirector for KTH Master programme inCybersecurity,a member of the Trust Working Group in theDigital Futures research center, and a faculty member of the Center for Cyber Defense and Information Security (CDIS). Mathiasis a co-founder of foreseeti, a company developing cybersecurity management software, started within the European Institute of Innovation and Technology,InnoEnergy. He is also running Energiforsk's cybersecurity network for small and medium-sized energy utilities. Organizationally he is a member of theSoftware Systems Architecture and Security (SSAS) research groupwithin theDivision of Network and Systems Engineering(NSE) at the Department of Computer Science in theSchool of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science(EECS)atKTH.
