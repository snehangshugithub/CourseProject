Jackie enjoys working with students helping them improve on their resume writing and interview skills. Previously, Jackie has worked at the DAmore Mckim School of Business Co-op Department as a Recruiting Coordinator. In addition, she has had previous roles in the admissions and financial aid departments at other universities. Jackie earned a Masters of Science in Higher Education of Administration at Northeastern University and a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology at Framingham State University.
