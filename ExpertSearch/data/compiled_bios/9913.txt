Alumni Current Student Faculty / Staff First Generation Student Parent Student Veteran Transfer Student Vikings Fan PSU Roy Kravitz Roy Kravitz Senior instructor and Westside program director Office FAB 20-17 Phone (503) 725-9046 Email roy.kravitz@pdx.edu Contact me Roy is a Senior instructor and the director of the ECE Westside program. He has created and co-taught courses in FPGA design, digital systems design, embedded systems, and computer architecture for more than 25 years. Roys professional career spans 35 years in multiple companies and roles. He started as a microprocessor designer at Intel and managed engineering teams and projects at RadiSys and Serveron. Roy earned his BS Computer Engineering and MS Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois, Urbana. His interests include embedded system hardware and software, Android programming, digital system design using programmable logic, and computer architecture.
