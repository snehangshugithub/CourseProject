Designing Computer Science Education, Fr 10:00AM - 11:59AM, Social Sciences Building 166 Biography Pamela Fox is a lecturer in the CS department, focused on classes in programming, software engineering, and CS education. Before lecturing for Berkeley, Pamela developed the computer programming and AP CSP courses for Khan Academy. She was also one of the first engineers at Coursera and one of the first developer advocates at Google. Pamela loves teaching, learning, and creating.
