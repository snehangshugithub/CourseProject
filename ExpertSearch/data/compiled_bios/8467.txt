Given Harper, Ph.D. George C. and Ella Beach Lewis Endowed Chair of Biology Education 1980 B.S. Biology, Transylvania University 1983 M.S. Zoology, Western Illinois University 1990 Ph.D. Biological Sciences, Illinois State University Research Dr. Harper is an ecologist with diverse research interests. He is currently supervising students who are investigating the distribution and habitat preferences of American Badgers (Taxidea taxus) and Plains Pocket Gophers (Geomys bursarius) in McLean County, Illinois. In addition, he is supervising students who are determining the species of breeding birds and their habitat preferences within the city limits of Bloomington-Normal, Illinois.
