Prior to joining Northeastern in the Mechanical and Industrial Engineering department, he was a Charles Ringrose Assistant Professor of electrical and computer engineering at the University of WisconsinMadison. Dr. Lessard was born in Toronto, Canada. He completed his B.A.Sc. in Engineering Science from the University of Toronto, and received the MS and PhD in Aeronautics and Astronautics from Stanford University. He also did postdocs at Lund University (Sweden) and at Berkeley.
