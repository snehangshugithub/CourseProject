Engineering, Liaoning Technical University, China About Shue Dr. Shue Wang joined University of New Haven in January 2019. Prior to that, she worked at the University of Michigan as a research fellow from 2015 to 2018. She received her Ph.D. in mechanical engineering at the University of Arizona in 2015. Her research areas include biosensors, single cell analysis, regenerative medicine and tissue engineering, mechanobiology, lab-on-a-chip, synthetic biology, and applying micro-engineered tools to understand the complex biological systems at both the molecular and cellular levels.
