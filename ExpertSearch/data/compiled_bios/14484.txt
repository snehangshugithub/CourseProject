I do my research at the Division of Communication Systems and Network Systems Laboratory ( NSLab ) under the supervision of Professor Dejan Kostic and Professor Gerald Q. Maguire Jr. My research area focused on different aspects of the next-generation data center. I'm currently profoundly interested in boosting the performance of the current DC system toward realizing the next-generation high-performance cloud. I am also a Senior researcher at Ericsson ABwith broad and in-depth knowledge of performance modeling, networking, virtualization, cloud infrastructure, and virtualized network function with an eye toward boosting performance.
