Professor Affiliations: Curriculum for the Environment and Ecology mitchell@bio.unc.edu 411 Coker Hall Lab Website My research group investigates the community ecology of infectious disease. We study pathogens infecting wild plants, chiefly grasses. Our main current interest is interactions between fungal pathogens and the broader leaf microbiome.
