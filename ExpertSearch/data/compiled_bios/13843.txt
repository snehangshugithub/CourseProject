Enter Search Below Search Profile Yali Amit Professor Departments of Computer Science and Statistics Email: amit@marx.uchicago.edu Phone: (773) 702-6614 Office: Jones 218 Website: http://galton.uchicago.edu/~amit Research: computer vision, image analysis, speech recognition Research Computer vision, image analysis and speech recognition: Object detection, recognition and model registration algorithms in digital images and acoustic data using hierarchies of templates. Applications to optical character recognition, zip code reading, face detection and recognition, automatic anatomy identification in medical images, detection and recognition of acoustic signals. Computational efficiency is emphasized. Statistical modeling and analysis of the data for understanding the performance of the algorithms. Parallel and biologically plausible neural architectures for implementing these algorithms involving interactions with research on biological visual and acoustic pattern recognition.
