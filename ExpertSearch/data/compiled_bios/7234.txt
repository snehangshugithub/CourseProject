Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, Seoul National University, Seoul, South KoreaResearch InterestsThe research in our lab includes (i)multi-length scale surface fabrication(nano-, micro-, and milli- scale manufacturing), (ii)thermal-fluids engineering(fluid mechanics, phase change heat transfer, mass diffusion, and droplet/bubble/particle-surface interaction), and (iii)bio-inspired surface engineering for a sustainable future(water collection, environmental pollution reduction, and energy-saving surface designs).
