jia@txstate.edu Phone: 512.245.3445 Research Interest Combinatorial Number Theory, Graph Theory, and Theoretical Computer Science. Professor Jia received his Ph.D. from the Graduate Center of the City University of New York in 1990. Currently, Professor Jia's research interests include: combinatorial number theory, graph theory, and computer network designs. Recently, he has been working on domination problems in graph theory and the routing problems in wireless networks.
