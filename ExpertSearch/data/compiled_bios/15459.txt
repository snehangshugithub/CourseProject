Enter Search Below Search Profile Aaron Elmore Assistant Professor Department of Computer Science Email: aelmore@cs.uchicago.edu Phone: (773) 702-6614 Office: JCL 241 Website: http://people.cs.uchicago.edu/~aelmore/ Research: cloud computing, databases, distributed systems Biography Aaron's research interests include elastic databases, database multitenancy (Database-as-a-Service), live data migration, cloud computing, and making data management easier for other scientific domains through collaborative analytic platforms and federating multi-database systems. Aaron was a Postdoctoral Associate at MIT, working with Mike Stonebraker on elastic and multitenant database systems, and Sam Madden on the DataHub project. Aaron's thesis on Elasticity Primitives for Database-as-a-Service was completed at the University of California, Santa Barbara under the supervision of Divy Agrawal and Amr El Abbadi. Prior to receiving a PhD, Aaron spent several years in industry and completed an MS at the University of Chicago. The University of Chicago Department of Computer Science 5730 S.
