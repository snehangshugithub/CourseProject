Visit Campus College of Science and Health > Research > Faculty Research Faculty Research Main Content Research plays an important role in the College of Science and Health. Virtually all of the college's full-time, tenured and tenure-track faculty maintain active scholarly programs that are supported by internal and external funding. Many faculty welcome student participation in their research laboratories. These research experiences complement the content of the college's robust graduate and undergraduate academic programs.
