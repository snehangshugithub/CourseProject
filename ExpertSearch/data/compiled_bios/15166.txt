Personal profile Research interestsProfile Dr Kang Ma was appointed Lecturer in the Department of Electronic & Electrical Engineering in 2016. Research Dr Mas research interests include low voltage (415V, LV) distribution networks, especially phase imbalance in LV networks. He is also interested in the planning and operation of distribution systems. For detailed descriptions of Dr Kang Ma's research areas and academic profile, please visit hispersonal website.
