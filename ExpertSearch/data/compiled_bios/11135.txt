He conducts research in financial markets, banking and institutions, and corporate finance. He has published academic papers in high quality Finance journals including Journal of Finance, Journal of Financial Intermediation, Journal of Corporate Finance, Journal of Financial Stability, Journal of Banking and Finance, and Journal of International Financial Markets, Institutions & Money. Prior to his PhD education at University of Kansas, Torna was a financial analyst in a major investment bank in Europe. He has been panelist at various national and international conferences and recipient of outstanding paper award in financial market and institutions by Southern Finance Association.
