She is currently a Senior Lecturer, teaching Biomedical Signal & Image Processing and Principles and Practice of Assistive Technology. As Director of Education for IMES/HST, Dr. Greenberg serves as Graduate Officer. She oversees educational policy, curriculum development, and academic affairs for all HST PhD students and coordinates with colleagues at Harvard regarding the training of HST MD students. She collaborates with faculty committees and mentors staff responsible for admissions, recruiting, student financial support, subject offerings, student records database, academic events, and the HST website.
