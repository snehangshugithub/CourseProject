His work is motivated by practical problems and usually involves combined experimental, analytical and numerical efforts. He has published more than 200 technical articles and reports, has co-authored one book, co-edited five books, and has lectured extensively both nationally and internationally.He was a 1997 ASME Fellow and in 2007 he was elected into the National Academy of Engineering.He has served on the Cockrell School of Engineering faculty since 1980.
