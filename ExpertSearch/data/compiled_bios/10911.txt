Research Areas High Energy Experiment Career History Ph.D., University of Michigan, 1966Research Associate, University of Michigan, 1966-1967Assistant Professor, University of California, Davis, 1967-1974Associate Professor, University of California, Davis, 1974-1981Professor, University of California, Davis, 1981-Present Honors 2013 European Physical Society High Energy and Particle Physics Prize, for an outstanding contribution to High Energy Physics, awarded to the ATLAS and CMS collaborations, for the discovery of a Higgs boson, as predicted by the Brout-Englert-Higgs mechanism.2019 High Energy and Particle Physics Prize of the EPS for an outstanding contribution to High Energy Physics, awarded to the CDF and D0 Collaborations, for the discovery of the top quark and the detailed measurement of its properties.
