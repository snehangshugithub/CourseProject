About Loyola's Single Sign-on (SSO) Loyola uses SSO: a service which allows users to provide their username and password once to a trusted service to have their identity securely and seamlessly provided to some of its web applications. To Log Out: Close your browser. It is very important that you log out of SSO when you have finished using the computer, particularly if the computer is in a public place. If you don't logout then subsequent users of the computer may be able to access applications as you. Login instructions: Use your Loyola UVID and password to connect. If you experience any problems, please clear your browser cache and try again.
