As a MechSE undergraduate, Kimberly Slaughter (BSME 15) created a powerful video for a class featuring women students in engineering, many of them from MechSE. The message of her video, which has been viewed thousands of times on YouTube, suggests that every young girl should be given the chance to excel in engineering, math, and science.
