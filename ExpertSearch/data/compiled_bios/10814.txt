Amjad Nusayr is a lecturer of Computer Science at UHV. He has been teaching Computer Science courses for over 8 years in different venues. His research interests are in Software Engineering, in particular in dynamic analysis, runtime monitoring, and Aspect Oriented Programming. He also participates in projects involving arduino programming and NXT mindstorrms.
