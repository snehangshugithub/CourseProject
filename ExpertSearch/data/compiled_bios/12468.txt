Cann 303D Dearborn Hallcann@engr.orst.eduPhone:541.737.9623Mechanical, Industrial & Manufacturing EngineeringProfessor of Materials Science Research Lab:Electroceramics Research GroupMaterials Editor: Journal of Materials Science Education: PhD, Materials, The Pennsylvania State University, 1997 MS, Materials, The Pennsylvania State University, 1993 BS, Materials Engineering, Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University, 1991 Research Interests: The electronic properties of ceramics, including dielectric properties, piezoelectricity, defect chemistry, crystal chemistry, non-stoichiometry, synthesis methods, and solid-state phase transformations. Applications include capacitors, energy storage, piezoelectric sensors and transducers, transparent conductors, chemical sensors, and interfacial phenomena. Biography: Assistant and Associate Professor, Materials Science and Engineering, Iowa State University, 19972005. Dr. Cann is affiliated with the interdisciplinary Materials Science Graduate Program at Oregon State. At Oregon State since 2005.
