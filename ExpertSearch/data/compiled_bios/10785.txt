Education B.S. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering, Mississippi State University About Muhammad Aminul Muhammad Aminul Islam received his B.Sc. degree from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology and Ph.D. from Mississippi State University. Currently, he is an Assistant Professor with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering and Computer Science at the University of New Haven, Connecticut, USA. His research interests include deep learning, computer vision, information fusion, autonomous driving, and remote sensing. Publications Book Chapter D. T. Anderson, G. J. Scott, M. Islam, B.
