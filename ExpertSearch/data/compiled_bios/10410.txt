His research interests are in ancient Greek philosophy. His papers include The Argument from Relatives (OSAP 2012) and Aristotle and the Ancient Puzzle about Coming to Be (OSAP 2015). His book, Aristotle and the Eleatic One, was published by Oxford University Press in 2019. Current projects include articles on Platos Republic and on topics in Aristotelian metaphysics and psychology.
